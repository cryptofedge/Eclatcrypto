1. **Post the teaser tweet** an hour before you mint. Build a little anticipation.
2. **Mint on Pump.fun** — follow `MINTING-GUIDE.md` (10 min)

4. **Update everywhere simultaneously:**

   - [ ] X bio + pinned launch tweet (paste CA)
   - [ ] Telegram welcome message (paste CA)
   - [ ] Pump.fun page's socials are already linked (you entered them during mint)
5. **Post the launch tweet** with the CA and Pump.fun link
6. **Drop the CA in Telegram**, tag everyone who pre-registered
7. **Post in Solana meme coin chat rooms:**
   - r/solana on Reddit (carefully — no spam)
   - Solana meme coin Telegram/Discord groups
   - Twitter replies under Pump.fun announcements
8. **Watch the bonding curve.** First few hours are make-or-break.

Graduation = $69k market cap = LP auto-locks forever. This is your goal.

## Red flags to avoid

- **Dev dumping** — if you sell your dev bag early, holders watch Solscan. It's visible in seconds.
- **Fake socials** — buying followers is obvious. 10k followers + 3 likes per post = dead.
- **Promises** — never promise price, return, or utility. Meme = no promises.
- **Ghost behavior** — if you don't show up in Telegram daily the first month, coin dies.
- **Multiple dev wallets loaded at launch** — looks like a coordinated dump. Use one dev wallet, keep the buy small.

## Legal reality check

- Stay in the "meme coin, no promises" lane. US SEC has gone after creators who promised utility or returns.
- Don't pay influencers to shill without FTC-compliant disclosure.
- If you have an identifiable real-world identity attached and you're in a strict jurisdiction (EU, UK, Germany, Japan, parts of the Middle East), a 15-minute chat with a crypto lawyer before you launch is cheap insurance.
